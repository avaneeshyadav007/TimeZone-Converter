ℹ️ About DST (Daylight Saving Time)

DST is the practice of advancing clocks during warmer months so that darkness falls at a later clock time. When enabled, the toggle adds or subtracts one hour from the standard time offset.

  • Spring Forward: Clocks move forward 1 hour (lose 1 hour)
  • Fall Back: Clocks move back 1 hour (gain 1 hour)
  • Note: Not all regions observe DST. The app automatically detects DST for supported time zones.
